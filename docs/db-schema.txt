Users
=====
username(PK), type(FK UserTypes), name, password, age, minutes

UserTypes
=========
id(PK), name(ex, GuestUser, AnonymousUser, LibraryUser)

Clients
=======
hwaddr(PK), ipaddr, name, owner(FK Level), shorttime BOOL

Level
=====
id(PK), type(FK LevelTypes), name, owner(FK Level) OPTIONAL

LevelTypes
==========
id(PK), name (ex: Organization, Branch, Department)

LevelAdmins (LT)
===========
level(FK Level), admin(FK Admin) (level+admin: compound PK)

Admins
======
id(PK), username UNIQUE?, password, password_salt

Options
=======
id(PK), opening_hours(FK YearHours), homepage, printer, agelimit(FK AgeLimit),
minutes, short_minutes

AgeLimit
========
low, high (high+low: compound PK)

YearHours
=========
id(PK), opening_hours(FK OpeningHours)

WeekExceptions (LT)
==============
year(FK YearHours), exception_hours (FK ExceptionHours) (year+exception_hours: compound PK)

ExceptionHours
==============
weeknr, hours (FK OpeningHours)

OpeningHours
============
id(PK),
monday, tuesday, wednsday, thursday, friday, saturday, sunday (all days FK Hours)

Hours
========
from, to, closed BOOL (from+to+closed: compound PK)
